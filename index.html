Principal's Message

Dear Students,

With an aim to preserve the culture of ultimate learning to the highest level of transaction through the "Guru-Shishya Prampara", Gurukul Bharti Educational Society Bilaspur H.P. is undertaking the process of establishing : Gurukul Bharti College of Education "at Village Benla Brahmana Distt. Bilaspur H.P. The sole objective of the college will be to provide an opportunity to the budding youths of the society to build a bright career in the noble profession of teaching. In the first step of this journey towards the enlightment all arrangements like physical, infrastructural, instructional facilities, have been made and the process of recognition/permission from NCTE for B.Ed. with intake capacity of 100 students, is in final stage.

Gurukul Bharti College of Education is being established by Gurukul Bharti Educational Society Village Benla Brahmana P.O. Chandpur Distt. Bilaspur H.P. It is registered Society under Society Act. with an objective to work for educational upliftment of society and development of manpower in society through education.
